What is this?

This is an album called Former Teenage Big Deal by Logan, from the Internet!. It was created and released in 2025. It is an odd album, created with digital instruments, in the style of the music that was common in 2006 -- namely guitars, screaming, and double-bass pedals.

It features screaming, dead memes, comments on the cruelty of a neighbor in 55-and-older neighborhood, post-doomer absurdism, a cover of an AI girly pop song, a cover a Y2K girly pop song, discussions on polyamory, and a reminder of that one time that Joey Chestnut publicly choked a guy out.
